# 🎤 Browser Speech Recognition - No FFmpeg Needed!

## ✅ **Solution Implemented!**

I've replaced the file-based recording with **Web Speech Recognition API** - now you can do speech-to-text **directly in the browser** with **NO FFmpeg required**!

---

## 🚀 **How It Works Now:**

### **✨ Real-Time Speech Recognition**
- Click **"Start Listening"**
- **Speak** into your microphone
- See **real-time transcription** as you speak!
- Click **"Stop Listening"** when done
- **No files, no FFmpeg, no backend processing!**

---

## 🎯 **Features:**

✅ **Real-time transcription** - See text as you speak  
✅ **Continuous listening** - Speaks as long as you want

✅ **Final results** - Shows confirmed text (green box)  
✅ **No FFmpeg** - Pure browser technology

## 💡 **How to Use:**

1. **Refresh browser** (Ctrl+F5)
2. Go to **Speech to Text** section
3. Click **"🎙️ Start Listening"**
4. Allow microphone access (browser will ask)
5. **Speak clearly** into your microphone
6. Watch text appear **in real-time**!
7. Click **"⏹️ Stop Listening"** when done
8. See final transcription! ✨
